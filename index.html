System Design Engineer focused on embedded systems, communication systems, and real-time signal processing. I build end-to-end hardware–software systems — from PCB layout and firmware to telemetry pipelines and custom ground stations — with a relentless focus on latency, reliability, and measurable performance.

My work spans adaptive DSP on microcontrollers, CubeSat attitude control with SDR communication links, RSSI-based localization and occupancy estimation, RTOS multi-core architectures, and analog communication hardware.

Currently pursuing a Bachelor's in Electronics and Communication Engineering at Madras Institute of Technology and conducting active research at Anna University on wireless signal-based occupancy estimation.
